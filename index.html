Security checkup — suggestions

How do I enable two-step verification?

+
Go to Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication. Use an authenticator app for better protection.

What if I lose access to my email or phone?

+
Use recovery codes, contact support, and update your recovery details regularly.

How to spot phishing messages?

+
Check sender, links, and spelling. Never share passwords via DMs.

Someone else is using my account — now what?

+
Change your password, log out other devices, and enable two-step verification immediately.
